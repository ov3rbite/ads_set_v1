Questions

Is SameSky therapy?
No. SameSky is peer support — connecting you with people who share your experience. It's a complement to therapy, not a replacement. If you're in crisis, we'll connect you with professional resources.
How are matches made?
We match based on the life experience you describe, not demographics. Our matching considers the specifics of your situation, where you are in the journey, and what kind of support you're looking for.
Can I stay anonymous?
Absolutely. You can use a pseudonym, share only what you're comfortable with, and reveal your identity on your own terms. Many members stay anonymous indefinitely.
What if my situation changes?
Life evolves, and so does SameSky. Update your profile anytime and we'll find you new constellations that match where you are now.
How are groups moderated?
Every constellation has a trained facilitator who ensures the space stays supportive, inclusive, and on-topic. We have zero tolerance for harassment or judgment.
