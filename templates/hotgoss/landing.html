FAQ

Can people really not screenshot?
HotGoss uses OS-level screen capture protection. On iOS and Android, screenshots and recordings are blocked. If someone tries, you're notified.
What does it cost?
HotGoss is $4.99/month for the full experience — screenshot blocking, auto-delete, custom reactions, and end-to-end encryption. No hidden tiers, no upsells.
How is this different from Signal?
Signal is built for security. HotGoss is built for vibes. We have the same encryption, but with screenshot blocking, custom reactions, and an interface designed for actual fun.
Can I use it in group chats?
Not yet — HotGoss is designed for 1-on-1 conversations right now. Group chats are coming soon.
What happens when messages auto-delete?
They're gone. Truly gone. Deleted from our servers and both devices. We can't recover them even if we wanted to.
